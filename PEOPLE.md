# People

RealGram is built by two people who have worked together on this ecosystem
for close to three years — starting with Shahnameh Season 1, through the
REAL token/blockchain layer and the Realink VPN product, into RealGram as
the platform that unifies all of it under one identity.

## Khabat Setaei — [setai.no](https://www.setai.no)

M.Sc. Computer Science (AI & Robotics). Architects and builds the platform:
mobile app, backend, infrastructure, and the REAL-ID identity model that
everything else in RealGram resolves through. Sets product direction and
owns what ships.

## Dr. Nasrin Dadashi — [dadashi.no](https://www.dadashi.no)

Ph.D. in Accounting, plus two Master's degrees in Accounting and Business
Administration. 25+ years in banking at Bank Melli Iran — from accounting
clerk to branch manager — alongside an active research career: two books on
deep learning in finance and blockchain accounting (2025), and 8+
peer-reviewed articles on AI-driven financial forecasting, ESG integration,
corporate governance, and risk management.

That background shapes a concrete role in RealGram, not an honorary one:

- **Economy design.** REAL's token mechanics, reward ledger, and reward
  caps get reviewed against the same risk-management and financial-modeling
  discipline her research applies to real financial systems — not just
  "does the number go up."
- **Anti-abuse review.** Every reward path in RealGram (referral, spend,
  skill-based unlocks) is required to clear a cooldown/anti-abuse review
  before it ships. That review draws directly on her risk-management and
  fraud/governance background.
- **The idea challenger.** The person in the room most likely to ask "why
  would this actually hold up" before a feature is built, not after.
- **Beta and experimental testing.** Hands-on testing of experimental and
  beta builds before anything reaches real users — the last check before a
  release, not a formality.

RealGram is a joint project. Both names belong on it.
