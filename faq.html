FAQ

Questions, answered plainly.

No jargon, no hedging. If something's missing here, it means it isn't decided yet — not that we're not saying.

What is RealGram?

RealGram is one app built from six connected parts — Freedom (the connection layer), Shahnameh (learn & play), REAL Wallet, Messages, Clan, and Hakim AI — all reading from the same REAL-ID. It's not six apps stitched together with a shared login; it's one account, one identity, one economy.

What are Freedom, Shahnameh, REAL Wallet, Messages, Clan, and Hakim AI?

Freedom is the connection layer that keeps RealGram online on difficult networks. Shahnameh is RealGram's game, rooted in the Persian epic of the same name. REAL Wallet holds the one balance every reward across RealGram settles into. Messages are the direct and group chats RealGram started as, verified against abuse under the hood. Clan lets you grow a group with friends under the same identity. Hakim AI is a guide you can ask when something in RealGram is unclear.

Is RealGram affiliated with Telegram or any other messaging platform?

No. RealGram is its own independent client, not a reskin or an official client of Telegram, Instagram, WhatsApp, or any other platform. It doesn't use their logos or branding, and isn't endorsed by them.

What is REAL, and how do I earn it?

REAL is the one balance every part of RealGram shares, held in your REAL Wallet. You earn it by referring friends, playing Shahnameh, and other verified actions. Nothing is credited until it's confirmed server-side — REAL is never just a number a client makes up.

What is Shahnameh?

Shahnameh is RealGram's game — a living game rooted in the Persian epic of the same name — chapters, quizzes, and a tap-to-earn loop that feeds directly into your REAL Wallet. You can reach it from inside the RealGram app, through Telegram, or straight from a browser — different doors, same RealGram experience underneath.

Which languages does RealGram support?

English, Persian (with true right-to-left layout, not a mirrored skin), Russian, and Chinese — each designed on its own terms rather than one layout with the text swapped out.

How does RealGram handle my identity?

RealGram uses REAL-ID as the account identity across the whole ecosystem — it isn't tied to a phone number, and linking an existing Telegram account is optional, never required to play or earn.

How do I get RealGram?

RealGram is live now — download it from realgram.no. It's a direct install, not distributed through a third-party app store client.

Is RealGram free to use?

Yes, RealGram is free to download and use. Earning REAL is a matter of showing up and playing, not paying.
